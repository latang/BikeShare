Average Distance Traveled

To find the average distance, I calculated the distance traveled of each One-Way bike sharing ride (since Round Trip ended at the same coordinates) using the starting and ending latitude and longitude coordinates using the Haversine formula. I then took the mean of all those distances and found that the average distance traveled was 4900 meters.

Commuters

I interpreted this as anyone who is holding a regular access pass (meaning not Walk Up) would be considered a commuter. Following that, if we consider each trip to be to a unique ridee, the number of trips taken by commuters would be 91203.
